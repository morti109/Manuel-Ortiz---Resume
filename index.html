My name is Manuel Ortiz. I am currently a first year at UC Riverside. I am from San Diego, and I moved here in the Fall of 2021. I currently live at the dorms here at UCR.

As for my current professional experience, I can definitely add more to it. I have worked as a tutor for the subjects of Mathematics, Spanish, and History. I helped create problems so that students were able to practice their skills in these subjects. For tutoring in the subject of Spanish, I would try to solely speak Spanish. This would allow the student to practice their speaking skills, as this is arguably the most important skill for any language. For math, I would solve the problem myself first. I would show them the methods that I used, and then give the student some practice problems or get started on the assigned homework. For History, simply doing some readings and helping the students understand the material was quite helpful. This is some insight into some of my methods in tutoring.

As for clubs, I did Best Buddies in high school and I am currently doing Hermanos Unidos at UC Riverside. Best Buddies was a club meant to raise awareness for people with mental disbalities. We did some fundraisers and attended some events. Unfortunately, our team did not get to do as much as we wanted due to Covid-19. I was a member/treasurer of the club. I was in charge of handling the finances for the fundraisers that we were able to execute. On the other hand, I am currently a member of Hermanos Unidos. The club's mission is to encourage Latino students to continue their studies and be part of a community with similar interests/ideas. As I am currently a member, I do not have any special tasks. In the near future though however, I would be interested in being part of the steering team.
